Why This Matters

The global population is aging rapidly, creating unprecedented challenges and opportunities for healthcare technology.

Aging Population

By 2050, the global population aged 60+ is projected to reach 2.1 billion. This demographic shift demands scalable monitoring solutions that preserve dignity and autonomy.

Fall Risk

Falls are the leading cause of injury-related deaths among older adults. One in four adults aged 65+ falls each year, with only half reporting it to their healthcare provider [1].

Vision-Based Solutions
